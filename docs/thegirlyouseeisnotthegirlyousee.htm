The Girl You See Is Not The Girl You See

A girl you see-
Confident,loud, outgoing.
A fearless one-not afraid to speak truth.
Everyone sees her as the girl
Who is always laughing,fun-loving.
But the girl you see fearless
have fear in her heart.
A girl you see always speaking
Has stories untold.
A girl you see outgoing
Feels the best at home-alone!
So,
The girl you see is not the girl you see.

~Pragni Naik
